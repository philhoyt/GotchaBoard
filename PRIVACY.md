# Privacy Policy — Save to Gotcha Chrome Extension

**Last updated:** April 2025

The "Save to Gotcha" extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any external server.

## What the extension does

- Reads the URL of images you right-click and sends it to your local GotchaBoard server (running on your own computer at localhost)
- Stores your GotchaBoard server URL preference locally in Chrome's storage on your device

## What the extension does NOT do

- No data is sent to any external or third-party server
- No analytics or usage tracking of any kind
- No cookies
- No browsing history collection
- No user accounts or authentication
- No remote code execution

## Data storage

All data remains on your local machine. The extension communicates exclusively with a server running on localhost (your own computer). Nothing leaves your device.
